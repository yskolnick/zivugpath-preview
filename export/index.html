Made for a WhatsApp Status.

A short, warm motion card a shadchan (or a friend) can post to their Status or a Story — her name draws on, one line, and a tap-through to the always-current profile.

  • Modest by default. Use an illustrated avatar instead of a photo — vibe and warmth without circulating a real image.
  • Ends on the link + QR. The motion is the hook; the real profile is one tap or scan away.
  • Same source, no extra work. Rendered from the profile you already built.

This is a live in-browser motion comp. In the product the card renders to a downloadable MP4 / GIF; the animation you see here is the real thing, not a placeholder.
