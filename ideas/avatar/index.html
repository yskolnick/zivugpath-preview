Output type · concept

An illustrated avatar — a modest alternative to a photo.

Not everyone shares a photo, and many shouldn't have to. A warm, stylized avatar carries vibe, dress, and energy — without a real image traveling through a dozen forwards.

Modest by defaultConveys who someone is without circulating a photograph — fitting where images are gated or withheld.
Vibe, not just dataFashion, warmth, and personality come through where a field-list can't.
Opt-in & labelledAlways the person's choice, always clearly an illustration — a stand-in for the photo, or a warm-up before it.
