What we keep

  • Deeply specific, warm, and a little funny — never generic
  • Interests, comfort things, humor, dreams, a daily mantra
  • Starter-pack callouts and light doodles
  • An illustrated avatar — modest, no real photo needed

What we cut

  • The "loving roast" — you don't roast someone you're presenting for marriage
  • "Slightly chaotic" energy — dignity wins here
  • A "weaknesses" headline — growth framing instead
  • The "healing journey" confessional — reads as baggage to a shadchan
