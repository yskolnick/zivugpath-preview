Part 4 · How it travels — and how it lands

One link. Every way people actually share.

The link doesn't replace the WhatsApp habit — it feeds it. And however a suggestion reaches someone, the reply routes through a person, never a cold message to a stranger.

One living link. Change a detail and everyone who has the link sees the update — no re-sending, no stale PDFs floating around.
