Format 1 · the default

Paste the link — it unfurls into a card.

Drop the link into any chat and it opens into a designed preview: her photo, her name, one warm line. Glanceable like an image — but it's a live link, so it's never a stale screenshot.

  • Her photo stays gated. The preview shows a tasteful portrait; the fuller gallery still opens only on the full link.
  • Always current. Update the profile once and every card already sent reflects it.
  • Zero effort. The card renders automatically — there's nothing to make.

Best forone-on-one chats with shadchanim and family — the everyday send.

Format 2 · the picture

The one-pager, sent as an image.

Plenty of chats forward a picture, not a link. ZivugPath renders the same profile as a single clean sheet — photo, a few facts in bullets, one line in her voice — sized to read comfortably inside a WhatsApp thread.

  • Same content, bullet voice. The prose page and the one-pager come from one source — write once.
  • Survives the forward. An image looks identical no matter how many groups it passes through.
  • Carries the way back. The footer holds the link and a QR to the always-current page.

Best forgroup chats and gatekeepers who save images to show at the Shabbos table.

Format 3 · the text

Just text. For when only text arrives.

Some of the most important people in a shidduch read messages on a kosher phone, or have them read aloud. ZivugPath writes a tight text version — name, a few bullets, the link — you copy and paste as an ordinary message.

  • Nothing to load. No image, no preview needed — it reads on any device, in any chat app.
  • Written, not truncated. Composed in bullet voice from the profile — not a copy-paste of the web page.
  • Still ends at the link. Anyone who can open it lands on the live profile.

Best forkosher phones, older relatives, and the fastest possible reply.

Format 4 · the broadcast

A story card for a WhatsApp Status.

When a shadchan wants many eyes at once, they post to their Status. This is a short, warm motion card — her name draws on, one line, then the link and QR — rendered from the same profile as everything else.

  • Modest by default. Use an illustrated avatar instead of a photo — warmth without circulating a real image.
  • Ends on the link + QR. The motion is the hook; the profile is one tap or scan away.
  • Downloads as MP4 / GIF. This preview is the real animation, not a mock.

Best fora shadchan's Status — one post, their whole network sees it.

The file

And when someone wants a PDF — it's a real one.

The link is the living version. But some inboxes want a file: a shadchan's folder, an email to a rosh yeshiva, a printout for a grandmother. So ZivugPath exports a properly typeset PDF — not a screenshot of the page.

  • Dated, so it can't go quietly stale. Every export is stamped with its date — a file from March announces it's from March.
  • Typeset like the page. Same type, same restraint — it prints beautifully in black and white.
  • Points home. The footer carries the link and a QR back to the always-current profile.
